## ğŸ¨ The Workspace

The Workspace is where the magic happens. Here you can analyze charts and annotate your trades.

### Drawing Tools

- **Cursor**: Select and move elements.
- **Lines & Shapes**: Mark support/resistance, zones, and patterns.
- **Text**: Add notes directly on the chart.
- **Position Tool**: Calculate Risk/Reward ratios visually.

### Capturing Trades

When you are ready to log a trade, use the **"Add Chart"** button. You can upload an image or take a screenshot, then fill in details like:

- **Symbol** (e.g., BTCUSD, EURUSD)
- **Timeframe** (e.g., 5m, 1h, Daily)
- **Session** (London, New York, etc.)
- **Outcome** (Win, Loss, Break Even)
